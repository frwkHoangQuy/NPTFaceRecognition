# Tai Lieu Kien Truc FaceID Kiosk (Chuan C4)

## Tong Quan He Thong

He thong FaceID Kiosk la mot giai phap nhan dien khuon mat tren thiet bi bien (Edge AI), duoc trien khai tren vi xu ly Rockchip RK3588 tich hop NPU. He thong thuc hien toan bo quy trinh thu nhan khung hinh, tien xu ly, suy luan mo hinh RetinaFace va hien thi ket qua hoan toan tren thiet bi (on-device), khong phu thuoc vao may chu de xu ly hinh anh. Kien truc phan mem tuan thu nghiem ngat nguyen tac phan tach trach nhiem (Separation of Concerns) theo cau truc goi `com.facepod`.

Tai lieu nay duoc to chuc theo mo hinh C4 (Context, Container, Component, Code/Algorithm), cung cap bon cap do chi tiet tu tong quan he sinh thai den thuat toan xu ly du lieu.
